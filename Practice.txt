Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets is a 2002 fantasy film directed by Chris Columbus and distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures. It is based on J. K. Rowling's 1998 novel of the same name. 
The film is the second instalment in the Harry Potter film series, and was written by Steve Kloves and produced by David Heyman.
